Policies & Terms

Workplace Conduct & Ethics Policy

At Rupino Royals, we uphold the highest standards of professionalism, mutual respect, and workplace integrity. Every employee, contractor, and associate is expected to adhere to this code of conduct.

1. Workplace Ethics & Professionalism

  • Employees must conduct themselves with integrity, honesty, and respect towards colleagues and clients.
  • Any form of **harassment, discrimination, or misconduct** is strictly prohibited.
  • Professional behavior must be maintained at all times, including during **interactions with passengers and vendors**.

2. Zero-Tolerance for Harassment

Rupino Royals has a **zero-tolerance policy for harassment**, including but not limited to:

  • Unwelcome verbal, physical, or written conduct of a sexual nature.
  • Offensive jokes, inappropriate gestures, or comments.
  • Displaying offensive materials (including online and offline).
  • Inappropriate advances or coercion in any form.

3. Confidentiality & Complaint Redressal

  • Any employee facing harassment can file a complaint confidentially through HR.
  • The company will ensure a **fair and unbiased investigation**.
  • All complaints will be handled with **utmost confidentiality**.

4. Reporting & Protection Against Retaliation

  • Employees are encouraged to report any misconduct **without fear of retaliation**.
  • Any form of intimidation or retaliation against complainants will result in **strict disciplinary action**.

5. Disciplinary Actions

Violation of this policy may result in:

  • Verbal or written warnings.
  • Suspension or termination of employment.
  • Legal action, if necessary.

For any concerns, please contact the HR department at hr@rupinoroyals.com.

Terms & Conditions

By booking a flight with Rupino Royals, passengers agree to abide by the following terms and conditions.

1. Flight Bookings & Cancellations

  • All flight bookings must be confirmed with full payment in advance.
  • If a flight is canceled **more than 48 hours** before departure, a **20% cancellation fee** applies.
  • If canceled **within 24 to 48 hours**, a **50% cancellation fee** applies.
  • If canceled **less than 24 hours** before departure, a **100% cancellation fee** applies.

2. Passenger Requirements

  • Passengers must carry valid travel documents, including passport and visa.
  • Failure to present valid documents may result in denied boarding, without a refund.
  • Passengers must arrive at least **45 minutes before departure** for domestic flights and **60 minutes before departure** for international flights.

3. Baggage Policy

  • Each passenger is allowed **50kg of baggage** for international flights and **20kg for infants**.
  • Hand baggage must not exceed **55cm x 35cm x 25cm**.
  • Restricted items include:
    • Weapons, ammunition, and sharp objects
    • Explosives, flammable liquids, and compressed gases
    • Radioactive and toxic substances

4. In-Flight Regulations

  • Alcoholic beverages will **not** be served on board.
  • Smoking, including **e-cigarettes**, is strictly **prohibited**.
  • Photography and videography are **not permitted** while boarding or disembarking.

5. Flight Delays & Changes

  • Flight schedules are subject to **ATC approvals, weather conditions, and security regulations**.
  • In the event of **flight diversions** due to weather or operational issues, the additional costs will be charged to the client.
  • If a flight is delayed beyond **5 hours** due to external factors, it may be considered a last-minute cancellation, and cancellation charges will apply.

6. Liability & Force Majeure

  • Rupino Royals is **not responsible** for any loss, delay, or damage to baggage.
  • In case of **death or injury**, liability is limited as per international aviation conventions.
  • Flights may be canceled or rescheduled due to **natural disasters, war, government actions, or operational issues** beyond our control.

7. Security & Passenger Responsibility

  • Passengers must **not leave baggage unattended** and should only travel with their own belongings.
  • Valuable items such as **cash, jewelry, and passports** should be carried in hand baggage.
  • Passengers must comply with **airport security regulations and airline staff instructions** at all times.

8. Modifications to Terms

Rupino Royals reserves the right to modify these terms and conditions at any time. The latest version will always be available on our website.
